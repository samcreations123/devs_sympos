Hey Nerds...

Technical quiz is a type of quiz that focuses on testing knowledge and understanding of the current Technologies.
Rules and Regulations
1. Eligibility: The technology quiz contest is open to all students who are currently enrolled in an undergraduate or postgraduate program in a recognized college or university.
2. Team Composition: Each team must consist of two members. No individual entries are allowed.
3. Registration: Teams must register for the quiz contest through the symposium website or at the registration desk on the day of the event.
4. Preliminary Round: The preliminary round will consist of a written test, which will cover a range of topics related to technology. The top-scoring teams will advance to the final round.
5. Final Round: The final round will consist of multiple-choice questions, and the teams will have to answer the questions within a specified time frame. The team with the highest score at the end of the final round will be declared the winner.
6. Tie-Breaker: In case of a tie, a tie-breaker round will be conducted. The teams will be given a set of questions, and the team that answers the most questions correctly within the shortest time frame will be declared the winner.
7. Conduct: The quiz contest will be conducted in a fair and transparent manner. Any instance of cheating or unfair means will result in disqualification.
8. Disputes: Any disputes regarding the conduct of the quiz contest should be brought to the attention of the quiz master or the organizing committee immediately.
9. Prizes: Prizes will be awarded to the top three teams.
10. Decision: The decision of the quiz master and the organizing committee will be final and binding on all participants. No correspondence in this regard will be entertained.
11. Participation: Participation in the quiz contest implies acceptance of all the rules and regulations mentioned above.
